Gender Laws Reference

Hover over a primary law category to view specific sub-laws and acts.

Equal Pay Act

Prohibits sex-based wage discrimination between men and women performing under similar working conditions.

Pregnancy Discrimination Act

Forbids discrimination on the basis of pregnancy, childbirth, or related medical conditions in hiring or firing.

Anti-Harassment Regulations

Establishes employer liability for failing to maintain a workplace free from sexual harassment.

Legal Gender Marker Acts

Provides citizens the option to select a non-binary or third gender marker on official government documents.

Anti-Discrimination Ordinances

Protects individuals from identity-based discrimination in public accommodations and housing access.

Title IX / Equal Access Act

Protects people from discrimination based on sex in education programs or activities that receive federal financial assistance.

STEM Inclusion Initiatives

Mandates equal funding and resource allocation for encouraging underrepresented genders in science and technology tracks.

Violence Against Women Act (VAWA)

Provides funding and legal framework for the investigation and prosecution of violent crimes against women.

Gender-Affirming Care Access

Protects the right of individuals to receive medically necessary gender-affirming treatments without discrimination.

Maternal Health Protection Laws

Guarantees comprehensive healthcare coverage and job security during maternal leave periods.

Domestic Violence Protections

Domestic violence such as sexual, physical, or emotional abuse is prohibited and protected against by law.

Sexual Harassment Protections

Prevents and addresses sexual harassment in the workplace and educational settings.

Online Harassment & Cyberstalking

Specific legal provisions that address gender-based cyberbullying, non-consensual image sharing, and digital threats.

Data Privacy

Regulations that protect gender-diverse individuals from unauthorized disclosure of their identity or medical history by digital platforms.
