Our Mission in Nepal

End Gender Violence

Provide resources, support, and legal awareness to survivors and at-risk communities across Nepal.

Close the Education Gap

Ensure equal access to learning opportunities for girls and women in all regions of Nepal.

Empower Communities

Build local champions for gender equality who drive change within their own families and communities.

Change Narratives

Challenge harmful traditions and replace them with evidence-based understanding and mutual respect.
